QI Lin

I am an associate professor at Ocean University of China.

I received my Bachelor and Master degree from Ocean University of China in 2005 and 2008 respectively, supervised by Prof. Junyu Dong. I received my Ph.D. from Heriot-Watt University in 2012, supervised by Prof. Mike Chantler.

My research interests include computer vision and oceanic data analysis.
